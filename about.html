About Us

Men's Shoes is an American multinational association that is involved in the design, development, manufacturing and worldwide marketing and sales of apparel, footwear, accessories, equipment and services. The company’s world headquarters are situated near Beaverton, Oregon, in the Portland metropolitan area (USA). It is a major producer of sports equipment and one of the world’s largest suppliers of athletic shoes and apparel.
